In 2016 I was a first-year student at Yahya Kemal College Skopje. Accordingly, I graduated from Yahya Kemal in 2020. During the 4 years of high school, I took part in various competitions and seminars through which I learned more about different fields of science.

In 2016, I took part in the competition "Mesec na Naukata" with my project called "Kelvin's Thunderstorm" and I won the second prize.

In the summer of 2017 I went to the Czech Republic where I was part of the Discover Summer Academy. It was a nice experience, I met some wonderful people, gained a bit knowledge in astronomy and I also learned a few lessons on how to grow as a person.

When I was a second-year student or in 2018, I participated in the Genius Olympiad in New York. Together with my friend Marija, we did a project on how we can clean polluted water with carbon dioxide. With our project we won the bronze medal at the international competition.

In 2018, I also took a course in experimental biology and chemistry at the research center Petnica in Serbia. I did a lot of experimental work there and learned the basics of working in a laboratory.

Back in 2019, my friend Marija and I also took part in the competition EuroScience in Cyprus with the same project as we did in the US.
